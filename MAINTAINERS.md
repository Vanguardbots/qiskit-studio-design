## Becoming a Maintainer

We welcome community members who demonstrate sustained contributions and commitment to the project to join as maintainers. If you're interested in becoming a maintainer:

1. Make consistent, high-quality contributions
2. Participate in code reviews and discussions
3. Help with issue triage and community support
4. Express your interest to the current maintainers
